Our Greenhouse-Safe Packaging Process

We spent years testing and refining our custom delivery packages. Plants don't ship like ordinary retail items; they require breathing room, humidity, and shock protection.

1. Root Soil Moisture Lock

We wrap pots in organic damp moss layers and biodegradable sleeves to prevent water depletion and keep roots intact.

2. Bamboo Anchor Stabilization

Internal bamboo support stakes anchor the plant structure directly to the cardboard shell, eliminating vertical soil movement.

3. Thermal Insulation Shields

Double-walled bubble insulation keeps plants shielded from exterior winter freezes or summer heatwaves.

4. Ventilated Protective Tubes

Heavy-duty structural shipping tubes protect delicate stems and foliage from crushing while allowing carbon exchange.
