Visit Our Flagship Store

Located in the serene heart of Botanic Hills, our flagship greenhouse spans over two acres. Here is how you can find us:

By Car (From Downtown)

Take I-87 North to Exit 14. Turn right onto Avenue of the Americas, then follow the signs toward Botanic Hills. Parking is free for all nursery visitors.

By Public Transit

Take the Green Line Subway straight to the Botanic Gardens Station. From the station exit, it is a pleasant 5-minute walk north along the leafy pathway.

Cycling

A scenic bike path runs directly along the nursery perimeter. Secure, shaded bike racks are available right outside our main entry gates.

Common Inquiries

Find quick answers to common questions about visiting our greenhouses and purchasing custom arrangements.

Do I need to schedule an appointment to visit?

No, you do not need an appointment to tour our greenhouses! Feel free to drop in anytime during our normal visitor hours. Appointments are only required if you would like a private consultation with Dr. Oliver or to organize a group field trip.

Do you offer custom potting and shipping options?

Yes, we do! If you buy a plant and a pot at our store, our specialists will repot it for you for free with the ideal premium soil mix. We also offer secure shipping in temperature-controlled packaging directly to your doorstep across the state.

Can I host private workshops or botanical events here?

Absolutely! The Orchid Pavilion can be booked for private workshops, corporate events, and wedding photography. Please send an inquiry using the contact form above, selecting "Wholesale & Bulk Orders" or "General Inquiry" so our events planner can contact you.
