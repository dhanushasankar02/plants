Nurturing the Connection with Nature

Greenery was founded on a simple belief: that living spaces become thriving sanctuaries when enriched with plant life. We exist to bring nature closer to your home and mind.

Our Botanical Journey

How a small glasshouse project bloomed into a nation-wide green sanctuary movement

2018

The Seed of an Idea

Started in a single glasshouse room in Oregon, cultivating organic soil formulas and growing hard-to-find aroids for local indoor collectors.

2021

Flagship Storefront Launch

Opened our flagship boutique architecture greenhouse in downtown, combining rare plant retail, expert consultations, and custom potting.

2024

High-Tech Research Lab

Inaugurated our dedicated plant pathology and propagation science laboratory to isolate pests, formulate organic feeds, and ensure zero-disease.

State-of-the-Art Plant Pathology Lab

We don't just ship plants; we engineer healthy lifespans. Our lab isolates micro-pests, researches light absorption metrics, and conducts soil aeration trials.

Every specimen leaves our facilities with a certified health check, ensuring it transitions perfectly to the microclimate of your indoor rooms.

  • Certified pest-free root acclimation
  • In-house custom organic fertilizer testing
  • Custom grow light spectrum configuration

100% Sustainable Green Sourcing

Our commitment to the Earth is woven into every detail. We operate a zero-peat policy to protect valuable wetland peatlands, utilizing coconut coir and organic compost instead.

From biodegradable peat pots to energy-efficient greywater irrigation systems, we minimize environmental footprints at every growth cycle stage.

  • 100% biodegradable organic growth pots
  • Recycled greywater irrigation systems
  • Plastic-free protective shipping materials

Local Greening Initiatives

We believe botany should be shared. Through our "Grow-together" neighborhood program, we dedicate 5% of all plant yields to community gardens and public school library green spaces.

We also host weekly propagation swaps and community planting workshops, empowering urban dwellers to claim their green thumb.

Our Core Philosophy

Three simple values that guide every single leaf we grow

Flora Biodiversity

Preserving and propagating rare cultivars to keep domestic urban collections biologically diverse and resilient.

Natural Aesthetic Design

Integrating organic architecture into human living, balancing clean geometric structures with raw foliage.

Environmental Stewardship

Operating greenhouses with minimum energy footprints and training the next generation of plant caretakers.

Rooted in Growth

Every seedling we nourish is a reminder of our responsibility to the ecosystem. We grow so that your home can breathe deeper.
